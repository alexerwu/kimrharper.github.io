About

After recently completing my Master of Arts in TESOL (emphasis on Pedagogy/Linguistics/Second Language), I decided to apply my pedagogical and linguistic experience to data science. In the past, I approached language as a social phenomenon and developed curriculums that emphasized natural learning

Now I aim to use data science in the same that I approached second language learning while adhering to modern concepts in cognitive linguistics. I have employed research skills acquired from the MA program to ensure that the data science research I conduct, is significant and repeatable. Conclusions are quantifiable and broken down into marketable results.
